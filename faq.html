Frquently Asked Questions

How do I register?

Registration will open on February 1st 2016. For the full details, check the registration page.

Where will I sleep? What will I eat?

We don't know either! For further information go to the accomodation page.

Which services are included in the participation fee?

The participation fee includes:

Accommodation between 10th - 16th August (if you have booked with accommodation), breakfast 11th - 16th August (if you have booked with accommodation), lunch 11th - 15th August, dinner 10th - 15th August, coffee breaks, access to all events of the program: student and guest lectures, poster sessions, lab tours, workshops, Conference Opening, Conference Closing, IAPS Workshops, IAPS AGM, Welcome Reception, Costume Party, Nations evening, Open Stage, German evening, Physics slam, Farewell celebration, sports tournament, excursions (there is a small extra fee for some of the excursions which have to be paid together with the conference fee), welcome package and T-Shirt

If you have booked without accommodation you get no accommodation and no breakfast. All other services named above are included in the fee.

Is it possible to extend my stay?

Yes.

Obviously, you will have to pay for these nights by yourself. Any additional nights are completely independent from the conference and depend on the availability of rooms in the hostel. No rooms have been reserved by us, except for the duration of the conference.

Can you help me to get a visa for Malta?

Yes, we provide confirmation letters to be presented at the Maltese embassy, if needed. If so, please indicate it during the registration process. We can only send you the confirmation letter after we have received the full payment of the conference fee. Please also have a look at the website of the Federal Foreign Office.

Are only physics students allowed to attend the ICPS?

According to the charter of IAPS you have to study physics or a physics-related subject to attend the ICPS. PhD students in physics are also very welcome as well as physics students up to one year after receiving their degree. Nevertheless you have to be a member of the IAPS to attend the conference. Therefore you have to join a national or local committee of IAPS in your country/city of study. If no such committee exists you can become a provisional individual member of IAPS during the registration process.

When is the deadline for the submission of scientific contributions?

The deadline for all contributions is June 15th.

Can I change my selected excursion?

It is not possible to change your selected excursions. Most of the excursions have only limited spaces and are booked out. The prices depend on your choice of excursions.

Do I have to be a member of IAPS to attend the conference?

Yes, you have to be a member of a national or local committee of IAPS to attend the conference. Therefore you have to join a national or local committee of IAPS in your country/city of study. If no such committee exists, you can become a provisional individual member of IAPS during the registration process.

How can I get internet access during the conference?

If your home university is part of the 'eduroam' project, there will be Wi-Fi available for free on campus, where most of the scientific program is being held. In this case you can directly login with your eduroam account details. At the moment, we are working on a free Wi-Fi access on campus for all participants.

What is the Arrival and Check-In procedure?

We'll know soon.

When do I have to Check-Out?

We'll know soon.

Can I take part in the ICPS although I am younger than 18 years?

Unfortunately, the age restriction on attending the ICPS 2014 is very strict and we cannot make any exceptions. The age restriction is related to the fact that you are not of full age in Germany if you are younger than 18 years. Nevertheless, we want to encourage you to apply for the ICPS 2017.

Can you give me financial support to pay for my flight or the conference fee?

We already tried to keep the conference fees as low as possible. We are sorry that we cannot lower the fee for you any further. However, you can participate in the article contest held by jIAPS and win a refund of the conference fee.

How do I participate in the jIAPS article contest?

Write an article about a physics-related topic of your choice and submit it to jIAPS until June 30, 2014. The writer of the best article wins a refund of the conference fee. Please keep in mind that you still have to pay the conference fee beforehand, as the price can only be awarded to accepted participants. You can find more information about the contest here.

What are the terms of cancellation?

The terms of cancellation are as follows: cancellation until May 01st, 2016 - full reimbursement. cancellation until June 10th, 2016 - reimbursement of 50% of the participation fee. cancellation after June 10th, 2016 - no reimbursement.

We advise you to inform us of your cancellation as soon as possible.

My question is not on this list. What can I do?

Please have a careful look at our website, especially at the registration and accommodation section. If your question is not answered, feel free to write an e-mail to info(at)icps2016.com. For detailed questions, please check the contact page.
