The Malta University Residence is conveniently located 3.4 kms from the University Campus. It is situated on extensive private grounds with a large communal pool and within walking distance from the quaint town centre of Lija and main public transport routes. The Residence is well positioned for accessing major shopping centres and historical sites. There is also access to eduroam. ---
