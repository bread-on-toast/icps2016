Getting to Malta

Air Malta and other international airlines link Malta with the major European and North African cities. There are daily connections to London and Rome as well as frequent direct flights to Milan, Brussels, Paris, Frankfurt, Zurich, Athens, Tunis, Cairo, Tel Aviv and Dubai. The main (and only) airport in Malta is Malta International Airport. Visit the Air Malta and Malta International Airport websites for more information on how to get to Malta by plane.

Local Transport

The local public tansport system consists mainly of a bus service. The main bus terminal is in Floriana which services the rest of the island. Have a look at the Malta Public Transport website for more information. The bus fares are:

€2.00 - Single journey

€21.00 - Seven day Ticket

€15.00 - 12 Single Day Journeys

Taxi

A taxi service from the Malta International Airport to Valletta costs approximately €20 or less.
